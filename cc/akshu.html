AKSHAY KUMAR

8bit Akshu, whose real name is Akshay, is one of the best and professional Esports Player, Well Known Gamer, Streamer & YouTube Vlogger. He started his gaming channel on YouTube on Aug 2019. He is the only Player in India who played with 4 Fingers. 8bit Akshu played more than 3 Tier A majors and 20+ Tier B along with LANs of BGMI (PUBGM earlier) and huge number of unofficial and official events since 2019 along with minor Valorant Tournaments. 8bit Akshu is the only BGMI Streamer who beats all top Level players like Jonathan, Mavi, Mortal, Scout and Dynamo in 1v1 TDM Matches. Now he is playing for Team 8bit. BGMI is one of the most famous mobile games in India. With a thriving esports scenario, it also has many passionate players who are highly skilled in the battle royale game.
