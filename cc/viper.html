YASH 'VIPER' SONI

Soul’s Viper Real name is Yash Paresh Soni. He was born on 18 December 1998 in Mumbai, Maharashtra. He is one of the most experienced and popular Esport players in India. Currently, he plays for team Soul. Also, he is one of the founding members of team Soul. He has represented India at many Global level tournaments of Pubg mobile and BGMI. His Current team includes Mortal, Goblin, Akshat, Omega, Hector, Regaltos, etc. Soul Viper is known for his Versatility and his gunpower is off the charts.
